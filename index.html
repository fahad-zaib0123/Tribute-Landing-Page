Abdul Qadeer Khan

"Father of Pakistan's atomic weapons program".

Here's a time line of Abdul Qadeer Khan life:

  • 1952 - Immigrated to Pakistan.
  • 1952 - Moved to West Germany to study at the Technical University of Berlin.
  • 1972 - Earned a Ph.D. in metallurgical engineering from the Catholic University of Leuven in Belgium.
  • 1972 - Began working on uranium enrichment technology for URENCO in the Netherlands.
  • 1975 Dec - Returned to Pakistan with stolen gas centrifuge designs.
  • 1976 - Returned to Pakistan with stolen gas centrifuge designs.
  • 1987 - Allegedly made a deal to sell 50,000 centrifuges to Iran.
  • 1990 - United Nations officials discover a memo detailing Khan's offer of nuclear technology to Iraq.
  • 1997 - Begins selling centrifuge technology to Libya.
  • 2004 - Publicly admits to proliferation network and placed under house arrest.
  • Died - Khan died on 10 October 2021, at the age of 85
